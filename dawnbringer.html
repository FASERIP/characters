Dawnbringer

Sentient artifact, divine simulacrum, angelic android: Dawnbringer is tall and athletic, appearing as a well-proportioned young woman in her late teens. Her strong, slender build and noble face are somewhat androgynous, but still feminine, and vaguely elfin. She has bright, sky-blue eyes and golden-blonde hair falling in spiky tresses like the sun's rays, and usually wears a serious, thoughtful, and somewhat distant expression.

Her construct form is timeless and immortal and though she has nothing, she wants for nothing. She can merely exist to fulfill her purpose of defending the righteous and vanquishing the wicked. However, she is learning to live as human beings do, among them, and learn to see herself in a new light. She is kind and compassionate to those in need, but fierce and destructive to her enemies.
