TSR Marvel Super Heroes RPG Advanced — Evenstar

Evenstar

Stella Blake is a striking figure, not least because of her stature which is somewhat taller than average, framed by a naturally athletic build. Her wavy black hair flows freely, catching the sunlight in its dark tresses, setting off her dusky olive-bronze skin. The richness of her complexion speaks to her vibrant heritage, hinting at sun-kissed summers. The jewel of her features, however, is her pair of bright green eyes - a shade so vivid, it would make emeralds blush.

Upon transforming into Evenstar, Stella's athletic figure takes on an ethereal luminescence and a silhouette of cosmic elegance. Her costume, as if crafted from the very fabric of the cosmos, clings sleekly to her form, accentuating her powerful physique. It gleams with deep, celestial colors, swirling with patterns of nebulae, star clusters, and galaxies far beyond our own. The suit's neckline plunges in a V, edged with silvery trim that continues down the center, leading to a stylized star emblem at her waist. Long, form-fitting sleeves end in fingerless gloves, while her lower half is adorned with a mini skirt over leggings, a nod to the playful fashion of the times. Her boots, high and sturdy, are a practical yet stylish choice for a woman who walks between worlds. Finally, a hooded cloak, flowing and nebulous, completes her ensemble - its interior twinkling with stardust, an ever-shifting map of the cosmos she has become a part of. The overall effect is as if she's a humanoid constellation, a fitting tribute to her namesake, Evenstar.

Stella Lucia Blake's ascension to cosmic heroism was as awe-inspiring as it was unexpected. A star-gazing teenager with an already growing fascination for the mysteries of physics, Stella's life was forever changed when a celestial event of monumental proportions crossed paths with her earthly existence. A dying star, teetering on the brink of becoming a black hole, reached out across the vast expanse of the universe and chose Stella as its legacy. The star's essence, a concentrated mass of cosmic energy, fused with her very being, intertwining with her mortal essence and imbuing her with power beyond earthly comprehension.

The transformation wasn't immediate, nor was it painless. Stella wrestled with her newfound powers, the physical and emotional turmoil of adolescence amplified by the unimaginable energies roiling within her. Yet, she persevered, guided by a sense of responsibility and the burning curiosity that had led her to stare up at the stars. The cosmic gift had not only amplified her physical attributes but also her intellectual capabilities, enabling her to grasp complex scientific concepts beyond her years. As the dust of this celestial gift settled, Stella emerged not just a teenager but a beacon of cosmic power - the Evenstar.

In the midst of her otherworldly adventures, Stella's earthly life is rooted in academia and hard work. She's a dedicated physics student, her days filled with equations and experiments that strive to decode the mysteries of the universe. To balance her academic pursuits, she works at Nonna Maria's Pizza and Gyros, a bustling local eatery run by the charming duo, Konstantinos "Pappous Kostas" and Maria Theresa "Nonna" Athanasiou (née Aiello). Whether she's delivering pizzas or waiting tables, Stella's life is a tapestry of the everyday and the extraordinary, an intriguing juxtaposition to her life as Evenstar.

The duality of Stella's existence is mirrored in her personality. As a student and worker, she's diligent and determined, marked by a spirit of resilience. Her intellectual curiosity is insatiable, her mind always in pursuit of answers. As Evenstar, however, she embodies a different kind of energy - one of adventure and audacity, a thirst for the unknown. Her mannerisms reflect this dichotomy: as Stella, she's often quiet, her bright eyes lost in thought, but as Evenstar, she radiates confidence and authority. In her, the heart of a hero beats, echoing the courage of a celestial being, and the soul of a young woman trying to find her place in the cosmos.
