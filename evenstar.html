Evenstar

Somewhat tall, and athletic, with wavy black hair, dusky olive-bronze skin, and bright green eyes, Stella Blake, AKA Evenstar, was given the essence of a dying star on the cusp of turning into a black hole when she became a teenager, becoming the Alter Ego of the heroic Evenstar.
