Ashtaret

An ancient Demon from terrible realms beyond, Ashtaret is long since finished with the machinations of cosmic evil and pops in to the Earth Dimension from time to time to live her best immortal life in the dual-natured glamour and shadows of 1980's Manhattan. Vain, capricious, self-serving, whiny, pouty, manipulative, hedonistic, vindictive, and generally chaotic, though not particularly malevolent, Ashtaret is out for herself first, last, and only. She just wants to have fun. She has taking a liking to Julian Constantine and has made him the unfortunate target of her somewhat annoying affections. She seems to believe herself entitled to unwelcome houseguestdom in one of Greymount Manor's spare bedrooms and access to its amenities when she comes to the mortal world, claiming ancestral right from the original owner and insisting the estate exists at all by her own involvement.
